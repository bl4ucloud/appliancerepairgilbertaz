Fast, Affordable, and Reliable Appliance Repair

At Gilbert Appliance Repair, we understand how essential your home appliances are to your daily life. Whether it’s your refrigerator, washing machine, oven, or dishwasher, when something goes wrong, you need a trusted expert to get it back up and running quickly. Our team of certified technicians is here to provide you with top-notch repair services, ensuring your appliances are functioning optimally.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our technicians are not only skilled but also friendly and professional, making your repair experience as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing the right appliance repair service can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. Here are just a few reasons why Gilbert Appliance Repair stands out from the competition:


  • Experienced Technicians: Our skilled technicians have years of experience and are trained to handle all major appliance brands and models. They stay updated with the latest technology and repair techniques to ensure they provide the best service possible.
  • Same-Day Service: We know that appliance issues can disrupt your routine. That’s why we offer same-day repair services to get your appliances back on track as quickly as possible. When you call us, we’ll make it a priority to schedule a visit at a time that works for you.
  • Affordable Pricing: We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our goal is to offer high-quality repairs at prices that won’t break the bank. We believe that everyone deserves reliable appliance repair without the worry of excessive costs.
  • Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed: Your satisfaction is our priority. We stand by our work and offer a warranty on all repairs. If you’re not happy with our service, we’ll do everything we can to make it right.
  • Local Expertise: As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the specific needs of our Gilbert community. Our technicians know the common appliance issues that residents face and are equipped to handle them efficiently.

Our Comprehensive Services

At Gilbert Appliance Repair, we offer a wide range of appliance repair services to meet your needs. Here’s a closer look at what we can do for you:


Refrigerator Repair

Your refrigerator is one of the most important appliances in your home. If it’s not working properly, it can lead to spoiled food and added expenses. Our experts can diagnose and fix a variety of refrigerator problems, including:


  • Cooling issues
  • Strange noises
  • Ice maker malfunctions
  • Leaks and water damage
  • Temperature control problems

We work with all major refrigerator brands, ensuring that your appliance is repaired quickly and efficiently.


Washing Machine Repair

A malfunctioning washing machine can be a major inconvenience. Whether it’s a leak, spin cycle issue, or electrical problem, we have you covered. Our washing machine repair services include:


  • Diagnosis of washing machine problems
  • Repairing leaks and drainage issues
  • Fixing spin cycle and agitation issues
  • Addressing electrical and control panel problems

Our technicians are experienced in handling both top-loading and front-loading washing machines, ensuring that we can get yours back to working order in no time.


Oven and Stove Repair

Cooking is a joy for many, and a broken oven or stove can put a damper on your culinary adventures. We can handle everything from faulty burners to temperature inaccuracies. Our oven and stove repair services include:


  • Repairing gas and electric ovens
  • Fixing temperature calibration issues
  • Addressing ignition problems
  • Repairing stovetop burners

No matter what type of cooking appliance you have, our technicians are equipped to diagnose and fix the issue efficiently.


Dishwasher Repair

If your dishwasher isn’t cleaning properly or is leaking, our team can help. We offer comprehensive dishwasher repair services, including:


  • Identifying and fixing leaks
  • Repairing drainage and clogging issues
  • Addressing cleaning inefficiencies
  • Fixing control panel and electrical issues

We understand how much time and effort a dishwasher saves, and we’re committed to getting yours back to peak performance.


Additional Services

In addition to our core appliance repair services, we also offer maintenance and preventive care to help extend the lifespan of your appliances. Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs down the line and ensure that your appliances run efficiently.

How It Works

Getting your appliance repaired with Gilbert Appliance Repair is simple and hassle-free. Here’s how our process works:


  • Contact Us: Reach out via phone or our online contact form to describe the issue you’re experiencing with your appliance.
  • Schedule an Appointment: We’ll work with you to find a convenient time for our technician to visit your home.
  • Diagnosis and Repair: Our technician will arrive on time, assess the issue, and provide you with a clear explanation of the problem and the repair options available.
  • Quality Repair: Once you approve the repair, our technician will get to work, using high-quality parts and tools to ensure a lasting fix.
  • Follow-Up: After the repair is complete, we’ll follow up to ensure everything is working as it should and that you’re satisfied with our service.
